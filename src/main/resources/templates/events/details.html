Sobre o evento

Join us for the most anticipated samba event of the year! The Elite Samba Congress 2025 brings together world-renowned dancers, instructors, and samba enthusiasts for four days of intensive workshops, spectacular performances, and unforgettable parties.

This year's congress features special guest instructors from Brazil, including five-time samba champion Ricardo Monteiro and the legendary Carla Oliveira. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or an advanced dancer aiming to perfect your technique, our diverse workshop schedule offers something for everyone.

The congress culminates in our grand gala show on Saturday night, where professional dancers showcase breathtaking performances that celebrate the rich cultural heritage of samba. This is followed by an all-night dance party where attendees can practice their newly acquired skills.

Don't miss this opportunity to immerse yourself in the vibrant world of samba, connect with fellow dancers from around the globe, and experience the authentic rhythms and movements of this beloved Brazilian dance form.
